ElectroStat

A smart home project for the course pervasive computing, M7012E.

About ElectroStat

ElectroStat is a pervasive software solution that allows the managment, logging and manipulation of power consumption within a household.

This project is currently devloped by a group of four fourth year students from Luleå Tekniska Universitet under the department of Computer Science, Electrical and Space Engineering. The project is currently in its early stages of development.
